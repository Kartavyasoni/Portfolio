## Overview

Global Lens turns an overwhelming, fragmented dataset into something a person
can actually reason about — clusters that group similar economies, forecasts
that project where they're heading, and a dashboard to ask "what if?".

## From raw indicators to insight

The hardest part wasn't the modeling — it was the **data plumbing**. A FastAPI
ETL pipeline ingests 16k+ indicators, and a caching layer cut repeat-query
latency by 60%, which made interactive exploration feel instant.

## Segmentation & forecasting

- **PCA + K-Means** reduced dozens of correlated indicators into a handful of
  components and grouped countries into 5 interpretable economic archetypes.
- **ARIMA** models, validated with rolling-window cross-validation, improved GDP
  forecast accuracy (MAPE) by 12% over a naive baseline.

## Delivery

The whole thing ships as a **Dockerized Streamlit app**, so analysts can run
live "What-If" scenarios on the global clusters without touching a notebook.
