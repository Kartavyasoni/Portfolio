## Overview

The system treats the LLM as a **reasoning layer over retrieved evidence**, not
a source of truth. If the corpus doesn't support an answer, the copilot says so
instead of inventing one.

## Retrieval quality

Hybrid retrieval plus a cross-encoder reranker mattered far more than the choice
of base model. Good context in, good answer out.

## Guardrails

- Answers are constrained to retrieved passages and always cite sources.
- Low-confidence retrievals trigger a graceful "I don't have that" response.
- Per-document access controls are enforced at retrieval time.
