## Overview

The win here was **trust through transparency**: every forecast decomposes into
interpretable components, so planners understand *why* the number moved.

## Methodology

A rolling-origin backtest kept evaluation honest and prevented leakage. The
ensemble captured both smooth seasonality and sharp promo-driven spikes.

## Impact

Analysts went from arguing about numbers to testing hypotheses, which changed
how the team planned inventory.
