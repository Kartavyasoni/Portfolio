## Overview

This project was less about a fancier model and more about **making a model
survive contact with production** — versioning, serving, monitoring, and
retraining as a closed loop.

## Explainability

Every prediction ships with its top SHAP drivers, so a customer-success rep sees
*"usage down 40%, support tickets up"* rather than an opaque score. That made
the model actionable and earned stakeholder trust.

## Reliability

- Evaluation gates block promotion of any model that regresses on the holdout.
- A nightly drift check compares live feature distributions to the training set.
- Rollback is a registry pointer change, not a redeploy.
