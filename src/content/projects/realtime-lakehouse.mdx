## Overview

The platform replaced a brittle nightly ETL with a continuously updating
lakehouse. The guiding principle was **one source of truth, one codebase** —
streaming as the default, batch as a special case of streaming.

## Key decisions

- **Delta Lake** for ACID transactions and time-travel on object storage, so
  late-arriving data and reprocessing were first-class, not afterthoughts.
- **Medallion layering** to keep raw immutability separate from business logic,
  making lineage and debugging tractable.
- **Idempotent merges** keyed on event id so retries never double-count.

## What I'd do next

Introduce a streaming feature store on top of the gold layer so ML features are
computed exactly once and shared between training and serving.
